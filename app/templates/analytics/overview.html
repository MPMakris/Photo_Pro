Understand the Data: Views Per Image

Below you see the how individual images perform. The vast majority of the Flickr images analyzed recieved less than 70 views. Beyond that, however, a significant portion of images perform far better, receiving view totals in excess of 150 and even 300. These high-performers represent the most eye-catching pictures on Flickr. Can your submissions do as well?

Snap Like the Pros

A significant portion of Flickr's user base has pro accounts. The sample of images analyzed actually contain slightly more pro images than non-pro. The models here can predict whether a photo is submitted by a pro account. Does you photo look like it was taken by a pro?
