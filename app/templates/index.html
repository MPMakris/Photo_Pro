About PhotoPro.Science

PhotoPro is a suite of analytics tools for gaining deeper insight into your photos. Using Machine Learning, its pre-trained and validated models provide intelligent feedback on key metrics for the professional photographer, marketting manager, and amateur alike.

Try PhotoPro Now

PhotoPro is as a capstone project for the
Galvanize Data Science Immersive program in Seattle, May-August 2016.

Methodologies

PhotoPro is relies on Machine Learning to train intelligent models that can understand and predict what makes a successful photo that people want to see.

Using thousands of Flickr photos available to the public, and scraping their performance metrics (like total views, comments, tags, etc.) from the Flickr API, PhotoPro trains its models over many generations. These models are then used to predict similar metrics for any new photo.
